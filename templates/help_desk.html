Frequently Asked Questions
How do I file a complaint?
Log in and go to File Complaint. Choose the correct category, add your consumer number, and describe the issue clearly. You will receive a Complaint ID by email.
Can a submitted complaint be edited?
Yes, but only while the complaint is in Pending status. Once review begins, the complaint is locked to preserve the official record.
What documents should I attach for billing issues?
Upload your latest bill copy, a meter photo if available, and a brief note describing the discrepancy.
When should the Safety Hazard category be selected?
Use it for exposed wires, sparking equipment, fallen lines, or any situation posing immediate danger to people or property.
⚠️ Safety & Outage Guidance
  • Never touch broken, hanging, or fallen power lines — keep bystanders away and call 1912 immediately.
  • Switch off appliances and unplug sensitive electronics during voltage fluctuations or storm outages.
  • Dial 1912 for urgent electricity failure and hazard reporting — available 24×7, toll-free.
  • Keep your Complaint ID and Consumer Number ready when contacting support for faster resolution.
